BACKGROUND

Axxel Technology Limited is a wholly indigenous onshore and offshore, Oil & Gas, ICT and Engineering Company with a big reservoir of experts in different fields of engineering.

Incorporated with Corporate Affairs Comission on the 13th of March 2017.
